# **App Name**: Caffeine Coders Hub

## Core Features:

- Service Showcase: Display the range of services offered, focusing on web development, AI, and data science capabilities.
- Project Portfolio: Showcase successfully completed projects with descriptions and visuals to demonstrate expertise.
- AI-Driven Consultation Tool: An AI tool that helps potential clients define their project requirements and provides preliminary solutions and estimates based on their needs. This LLM will use reasoning to decide whether to incorporate certain technologies or frameworks in its analysis.
- Team Introduction: Introduce the core team members with their expertise areas to build trust and showcase the company's talent.
- Client Testimonials: Display client testimonials and reviews to add credibility to the services offered.
- Contact and Inquiry Form: Enable potential clients to easily reach out through a contact form or direct contact details.
- Blog/Insights Section: A section with articles and insights on web development, AI trends, and data science applications.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep blue (#34495E) to convey professionalism and reliability.
- Background color: Light gray (#F4F6F6), providing a clean and modern backdrop.
- Accent color: Bright green (#2ECC71) to highlight calls to action and important information.
- Headline font: 'Poppins', sans-serif, for a geometric, contemporary, fashionable look.
- Body font: 'PT Sans', sans-serif, providing a modern look and readability.
- Use modern, minimalist icons related to technology and services offered.
- Clean and structured layout with clear sections for easy navigation and information access.
- Subtle transitions and animations to enhance user experience and guide interactions.
